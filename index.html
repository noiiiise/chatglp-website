I just started Ozempic. What supplements should I take?
Great question! Many GLP-1 users benefit from a multivitamin, B12, and a fiber supplement to support digestion. Also consider protein supplementation to preserve lean muscle mass.
What about nausea? It's been rough.
Nausea is common early on. Try eating smaller, bland meals, staying hydrated, and ginger tea. It typically improves within a few weeks. If it persists, your provider can adjust the dose.
